02

Journey

⌄
1993
Founded by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem. Original wedge: graphics chips for PC gaming.
1999
NVIDIA launches GeForce 256 and popularizes the GPU as a category.
2006
CUDA opens the GPU to general-purpose parallel computing. จุดนี้คือ seed ของ AI moat.
2020
Mellanox acquisition adds networking, turning NVIDIA from chip seller into cluster/system supplier.
FY2026
NVIDIA scales Blackwell Ultra, reaches $215.9B annual revenue, and reframes itself as a data center-scale AI infrastructure company.

05

What do they Actually Do?

⌄

AI Data Center Infrastructure. NVIDIA sells accelerated compute platforms: GPUs, CPUs, DPUs, NVLink, InfiniBand, Spectrum-X Ethernet, full rack systems, and software.

Edge Computing and Graphics. GeForce gaming, RTX workstations, robotics, automotive, AI PCs, game consoles, and visualization remain important. แต่ตอนนี้ these are smaller planets orbiting the data center sun.

Software Layer. CUDA, AI Enterprise, NIM, Omniverse, Dynamo inference software, and domain libraries make NVIDIA harder to replace.

Big-picture frame: NVIDIA is the operating layer for accelerated computing infrastructure.

08

Current Situation & Positioning

⌄
Momentum

Blackwell is now the center of shipments

Q1 FY2027 revenue grew to $81.6B, and NVIDIA said Blackwell accounted for the majority of system shipments.

Momentum

Data Center is almost the whole company

Data Center was $75.2B in Q1 FY2027, more than 92% of total revenue by market platform.

จับตา

Customer concentration and capital availability

NVIDIA warns that data centers, energy, and capital availability are crucial. ถ้า hyperscaler capex slows or power/buildout constraints bite, revenue timing can move.

Risk

China export controls

At FY2026 year-end, NVIDIA said it was effectively foreclosed from competing in China's data center compute market. In Q1 FY2027 it had licenses for small H200 shipments, but no revenue under that program yet.

09

Peers and Competitors

⌄
AI Accelerators and Training/Inference
The main battlefield is performance per dollar, supply, ecosystem, and software maturity.
NVIDIA
Default platform for large AI clusters: GPU + networking + CUDA + systems + libraries.
AMD
Most visible merchant GPU alternative, especially where customers want second-source leverage.
Google TPU, Amazon Trainium/Inferentia, Microsoft Maia
Custom ASICs reduce dependence on NVIDIA for internal workloads.
Verdict: NVIDIA is still the reference architecture, but custom silicon is the serious long-term pressure point.
AI Networking
Cluster performance increasingly depends on interconnect and networking.
NVIDIA InfiniBand, NVLink, Spectrum-X
Mellanox made NVIDIA a system company.
Broadcom, Marvell, Arista, Cisco
Compete around Ethernet switching, custom silicon, and hyperscaler network designs.
Verdict: NVIDIA has structural advantage when customers buy complete rack-scale systems.
